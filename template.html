Opening January 2019

The first trans owned and operated retreat space in Colorado is opening soon. Like every trans space the funding is tight and any for of donation or support will be graciously accepted. All donations fund current projects.

  • Queer Homesteading Camp

    5 session camp offered to show the skills required to homestead from spring to fall

  • On site queer library

    fire proof structure to preserve our texts

  • Queer Art - installation space

    a permanent space for rotating art installations
