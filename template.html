Manifesto

Shared with me by a teacher many years ago, the quote below quides me: "A master in the art of living knows no difference between work and play, life and love, to them they are always doing both."

The Earth and humanity face challenges never seen before in known history. We must all work together to improve the health of the planet and human connection. I am commited to serving and loving all life on this planet and into the universes beyond. Through my efforts of being a servant leader and community member I aspire to leave evidence in living forms.
